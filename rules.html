🛡️ Server Rules

Play fair. Respect others. Help us build a fun and safe community.

🏠 Home 🗳️ Vote

1. Modifications

✅ Allowed Mods:

  • Client-side performance mods (e.g. Optifine, Sodium)
  • Aesthetic mods like shaders
  • Armor/effect status HUDs
  • Brightness/gamma adjustments
  • AFK autoclickers (for mob farms or mining only)

2. Behavior & Chat

  • 🚫 No hate speech, racism, or discrimination
  • 🚫 No spamming, flooding, or excessive caps
  • ✅ Respect staff and players
  • ✅ Use English in public chats when possible

3. Griefing & Stealing

  • 🚫 Griefing is not allowed in claimed or unclaimed areas
  • 🚫 Stealing from other players is forbidden
  • ✅ Use the /claim system to protect your base

4. Exploits & Hacking

  • 🚫 No hacked clients or cheat mods
  • 🚫 No abusing bugs or glitches
  • ✅ Report bugs to staff

5. PvP & Raiding

  • ✅ PvP is allowed in wilderness/unclaimed areas
  • 🚫 Combat logging is not allowed
  • 🚫 No TP trapping/killing
  • ✅ Respect fair play rules in PvP zones

6. AFK & Farming

  • ✅ Mending gear is allowed during AFK
  • 🚫 Auto-fishing scripts/macros are not allowed
  • ✅ Use /afk properly for AFK rewards

7. Staff Interaction

  • ✅ Follow all staff instructions
  • 🚫 False reporting or wasting staff time may result in punishment
  • ✅ If you disagree, report on Discord or open a ticket
